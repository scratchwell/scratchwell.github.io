TouchPilot Support

TouchPilot turns your iPhone into a lightweight trackpad and keyboard input surface for your iPad.

What TouchPilot Supports

  • iPhone-to-iPad pointer input over Bluetooth
  • Click, right click, drag hold, and edge scrolling
  • System keyboard, function keys, and custom shortcuts
  • Light and dark appearance

Important Notes

  • TouchPilot v1 is designed for iPad as the receiving device.
  • Mac and general Bluetooth devices are not currently supported.
  • Input stops when the app moves to the background.
  • Compatibility can vary by device and OS version.

Basic Setup

  1. Open TouchPilot on your iPhone.
  2. Allow Bluetooth access when prompted.
  3. Open TouchPilot Settings and enable iPad connection waiting.
  4. On your iPad, open Settings > Bluetooth.
  5. Select the TouchPilot Bluetooth name shown in the iPhone app.

Troubleshooting

  • If the iPad does not find TouchPilot, turn Bluetooth off and on again on both devices.
  • If a previous pairing exists, remove the old TouchPilot entry from iPad Bluetooth settings and pair again.
  • Keep TouchPilot open in the foreground while using it.
