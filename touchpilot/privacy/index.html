TouchPilot Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 23, 2026

TouchPilot is made by ScratchWell. This policy describes how TouchPilot handles information when you use the app.

Summary

TouchPilot does not use TouchPilot accounts, developer servers, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking. The app is designed to send keyboard and pointer input from your iPhone to your own iPad over Bluetooth.

Information We Do Not Collect

TouchPilot does not collect, store, sell, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or account information
  • Your typed text or key events on a ScratchWell server
  • Bluetooth device lists on a ScratchWell server
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, files, or microphone data

Bluetooth

TouchPilot uses Bluetooth to allow your iPad to receive keyboard and pointer input from your iPhone. Bluetooth permission is used only for the app's input-device behavior. The app does not send Bluetooth data to ScratchWell servers.

Input Data

When you use the trackpad, keyboard, function keys, or shortcuts, TouchPilot sends the corresponding input reports to the connected iPad.

TouchPilot does not send your input text or key events to ScratchWell. The app currently has no developer-operated server.

Settings Stored on Your iPhone

TouchPilot stores settings locally on your iPhone using Apple's system storage. These settings may include:

  • Trackpad sensitivity and acceleration
  • Scroll rail placement and scroll direction
  • Drag timing
  • Haptic feedback preference
  • Shortcut definitions
  • Tutorial completion state
  • Universal Clipboard preference

These settings are stored on your device and are not sent to ScratchWell.

Universal Clipboard

Some characters, such as emoji, cannot be sent directly by a standard Bluetooth keyboard report. If you choose to use the Universal Clipboard fallback, TouchPilot may place the text into the iPhone clipboard and send a paste command to the connected iPad.

Universal Clipboard is an Apple system feature. It requires the same Apple Account, Handoff, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and nearby devices. TouchPilot cannot verify whether the clipboard transfer succeeded.

Background Behavior

TouchPilot stops advertising, scanning, and sending input when the app moves to the background. The app is intended to work while it is open and active.

Third-Party Services

TouchPilot currently does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs. If this changes in the future, this policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated before release.

Children

TouchPilot is a productivity utility and is not directed at children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

ScratchWell may update this policy as TouchPilot changes. The effective date will be updated when the policy changes.
